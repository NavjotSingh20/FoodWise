Smart Auto-Prediction Recommended

Blends the standard adult portion (0.6 kg/student) with your hostel's actual historical consumption average, then applies a 10% safety buffer for reliable planning.

How the formula works
1Standard = Students × 0.6 kg (adult portion)
2Historical = AVG of all past consumed kg
3Blend = (Standard × 50%) + (Historical × 50%)
4Final = Blend × 1.10 (+10% safety buffer)
qty = ((students × 0.6) × 0.5 + AVG(consumed) × 0.5) × 1.10
